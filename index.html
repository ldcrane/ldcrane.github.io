Leland D. Crane

Principal economist at the Federal Reserve Board, working on labor markets, AI, and economic measurement.

Research CV Industry of the minute

I study the labor market and the macroeconomy using nontraditional data — payroll microdata, text, and large language models — with a long-running interest in search and matching.

This is a personal website. Any results or conclusions in the research presented here are my own and do not necessarily represent the views of the Federal Reserve System.
